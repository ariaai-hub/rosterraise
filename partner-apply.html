Common Questions

How do I get paid? +
We pay out commissions monthly via PayPal or direct deposit — your choice. You'll have a dashboard where you can see every team you've referred, their sales, and exactly what you've earned. Payments are sent automatically on the 15th of each month for the prior month's commissions.
Is there a minimum number of teams I need to refer? +
No. Refer one team or fifty. There's no minimum, no quota, and no obligation. You earn on whatever you bring in — it all adds up.
How does the commission tracking work? +
Every team you refer gets a unique link or code tied back to you. When someone orders from that team's store, the system automatically attributes it to your account and adds to your commission balance. You'll be able to see real-time data in your partner dashboard.
What if a team cancels or stops selling? +
If a referred team goes inactive — no sales in 90 days — that team moves to an inactive status and future recurring commissions stop. But you keep earning on any other active teams you've referred. There's no penalty and no obligation to refer more.
What's the difference between first-order commission and recurring commission? +
First-order commission is the higher rate applied to a team's very first order — it's our way of rewarding you for bringing in a new customer. Recurring commission is the rate applied to all subsequent orders from that team, for as long as the team stays active. Both are paid to you for the lifetime of the account.
