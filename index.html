Questions
Frequently Asked
Questions
How long does it take to set up our team store? +
After you submit your logo and team information, your custom store is typically live within 48 hours. You'll receive mockup previews so you can see exactly what your products look like before anything goes public.
Is there a minimum number of items that have to sell? +
No. Unlike traditional fundraisers, you don't pre-sell, commit to quantities, or pay upfront. There is no minimum. Sales happen organically through your store, and your team earns on every item sold.
How much does it cost to get started? +
$0 to set up your team store. The sample kit is entirely optional — order only if you want physical products to show at games and parent events. Getting started costs nothing.
Do parents have to coordinate pickup or delivery? +
Never. Every order ships directly to the buyer's door — whether that's a parent, grandparent, aunt, or uncle. No coordinating bulky pickup orders. No storage in your garage.
Can we add more products after our store is live? +
Yes. We can add new products, colors, types, or designs at any time. You can also run limited drops for specific seasons, events, or tournaments.
Who handles quality issues and returns? +
We do. If anything is wrong with an order — wrong size, print issue, damage in shipping — we make it right directly with the customer. Your name never gets dragged into an unhappy conversation.
Do you ship internationally? +
Currently all products ship within the United States only. We're working on expanding, but for now we're US-only to keep production fast and reliable for every team.
