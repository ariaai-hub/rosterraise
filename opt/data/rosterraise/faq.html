How It Works
Getting Started
How does RosterRaise actually work? +
You sign up and give us your team logo, name, sport, and colors. Within 48 hours we build a custom storefront with product mockups made specifically for your team. You approve the designs, and your store goes live. Every time a parent or fan buys something, your team earns 30% of the sale. We handle printing, shipping, and all customer service. You just share the link.
How long does it take to set up our team store? +
Your store is typically live within 48 hours of submitting your information. You'll receive mockup previews so you can see exactly what your products look like before anything goes public. The signup form itself takes about 2 minutes.
Do we need design skills or a website? +
No. We design your store and create product mockups for you. You don't need a website, design software, or any technical skills. You just approve the mockups we send and share your store URL with parents.
How do we share the store with parents? +
You'll get a unique team store URL (for example: rosterraise.com/store/yourteam). You can share it via text, email, Facebook, Instagram, or send it home with kids on a flyer. We also provide ready-to-post social graphics and email copy so you can share in under a minute.
Products
What Parents Can Buy
What kind of products can we sell? +
We offer 200+ custom products including koozies, spirit chains, keychains, hoodies, t-shirts, backpacks, duffle bags, water bottles, decals, koozie wraps, and window clings. Every item is custom-printed or embroidered with your team's logo, name, and colors. No generic stock items — everything is made-to-order for your team.
Can we add different products after we launch? +
Yes. We can add new products, colors, styles, or designs at any time after your store is live. You can also run limited-theme drops for specific seasons, tournaments, or events.
Is there a minimum order quantity? +
No minimums. Unlike traditional fundraisers where you commit to bulk quantities upfront, every RosterRaise product is made-to-order. No pre-selling required, no inventory to manage, no upfront costs.
Payments & Earnings
How Teams Get Paid
How is the commission calculated? +
Your team earns 30% of the retail sale price on every item sold. For example, if a koozie sells for $5, your team receives $1.50. If a hoodie sells for $30, your team receives $9. The price is set by us and covers all production and shipping — you earn pure commission on top.
When and how do teams get paid? +
Team commissions are paid every 2 weeks via check or direct deposit. No invoicing, no chasing — we send earnings on a set schedule. You'll have a real-time dashboard showing every sale and your running total so there's full visibility.
How much does it cost to get started? +
$0 to set up your team store. The setup process is free. If you want physical samples of your team's products to show at games or parent events, you can order an optional sample kit — but that's entirely not required to get started or to earn commissions.
Shipping & Fulfillment
Orders & Delivery
How long does shipping take? +
Orders typically ship within 3–5 business days directly to the buyer's door. No coordinating pickup, no storage in your garage. Every order — whether it's to a parent, grandparent, or extended family member — ships individually with tracking.
Who handles customer service and returns? +
We handle every customer question, quality issue, and return directly with the buyer. Your name stays clean and your focus stays on coaching. If anything is wrong — wrong size, print defect, shipping damage — we make it right.
Do you ship internationally? +
All products currently ship within the United States only. We're working on expanding international shipping to keep production fast and reliable, but for now every order goes to US addresses.
Multiple Fundraisers
Running More Than One
Can we run separate fundraisers for multiple teams? +
Yes. Each team gets its own branded storefront with its own URL. If you're a club with multiple age groups or a league with many teams, each can have a separate store and separate commission payouts.
Can we run more than one campaign at the same time? +
Your store is always live, so you can run ongoing fundraising all season long. You can also create limited-time drops for specific events, tournaments, or senior nights — then rotate in new products. There's no limit on how many campaigns you run.
